# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge to create an environment of mutual respect, tolerance, and openness for all individuals, regardless of their background, identity, or experience level.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful** - Use welcoming and inclusive language
- **Being patient** - Understand that everyone has different experience levels
- **Being collaborative** - Work together constructively
- **Being supportive** - Help others learn and grow
- **Being honest** - Provide constructive feedback in good faith
- **Being diverse** - Appreciate different perspectives and ideas
- **Being humble** - Acknowledge mistakes and learn from them

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

We do not tolerate behavior such as:

- Harassment or discrimination based on protected characteristics
- Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, actual or perceived socioeconomic status, or caste
- Threatening, insulting, or dehumanizing language
- Deliberate misgendering or use of deadnames
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Nonconsensual photography or recording
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behaviors
- Sustained disruption of events or platforms

---

## Enforcement

Community managers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior.

### **How We Respond to Violations**

1. **Warning**: A private message explaining why the behavior is problematic
2. **Temporary Mute**: Temporary restriction from participation
3. **Permanent Ban**: Removal from the community

### **Reporting Process**

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document** the incident (screenshots, links, context)
2. **Report** privately to project maintainers
3. **Include**:
   - What happened
   - When it happened
   - Who was involved
   - Any context or links
   - If you feel safe sharing

**Email**: your.email@example.com
**Discord/Slack**: [Community Link]
**GitHub Issues**: Use template for sensitive reports

---

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior
- Investigating all reports fairly and thoroughly
- Taking appropriate action in response to violations
- Protecting the privacy of reporters
- Being transparent about consequences

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces including:

- GitHub issues, discussions, and pull requests
- Project repositories and documentation
- Discord servers, Slack channels
- In-person events and meetups
- Any space representing this project
